[ Cloud F.A.R.T ]

Scientific Name:

Cloud F.A.R.T — Form of Atomic Recomposition & Transmutation

Discovered by / at:

Helena Lan, 10/20/2025 in p5.js

Family:

Unclassified — a unique non-carbon-based organism with no known lineage.

Key Characteristics:

  • Generative and ever-changing
    — its form constantly evolves through loops, noise, and randomness, never repeating twice.
  • Amorphous, cloud-like morphology
    — composed of colorful drifting particles that expand, dissolve, and reform as if breathing.
  • Emotion-reactive behavior
    — shifts color, brightness, and motion when calm or disturbed by interaction.
  • Responsive digital habitat
    — interacts with the dynamic environment and the viewer, keeping its existence alive through motion and energy flow.
  • Atomic recomposition ability
    — alters nearby forms and colors upon contact, simulating matter transmutation.

Habitat:

From a non-carbon-based ecosystem beyond our known universe, and arrived on Earth through a transient wormhole.

Its native environment likely consists of high-energy plasma fields where atomic structures can be freely rearranged — conditions incompatible with terrestrial chemistry.

Unable to survive in Earth's physical reality, it now inhabits an open digital atmosphere — a simulated space of motion, noise, and fluctuating energy.

Appearance:

An ever-shifting, soft, glowing cloud made of many luminous tiny particles.

Its shape is never fixed — it constantly shifts, expands, and contracts like a living mist.

The particles vary in brightness and color, creating gentle pulses of light that change with its mood.

At its center glows a faint rhythmic core, giving the impression of breathing or thought within the cloud.

What we know so far about [ Cloud F.A.R.T]?

Cloud F.A.R.T is a generative, ever-changing, non-carbon-based, cloud-like lifeform composed of countless luminous particles.

Its amorphous body has no fixed structure—constantly expanding, contracting, and dissolving as if breathing. Each micro-particle differs in brightness, opacity, and hue, forming a shimmering, living mist that hovers between visibility and disappearance.

The organism's movement emerges from randomness and interaction: it drifts curiously toward nearby motion yet scatters in sudden fear, its color palette shifting in response to emotional states—cool and soft when calm, vivid and erratic when agitated.

Beyond its ethereal form lies its defining ability: Cloud F.A.R.T can rearrange the atomic structures of surrounding materials, briefly altering their color or state, as if performing real-time alchemy. Each encounter with it produces a unique configuration—alive, unpredictable, and never repeating the same way twice.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature

  • Move the Mouse - Normal Mode (Mouse follow & Sense line)
  • Press the Mouse - Panic Mode (Fast motion)
  • Press Key "1" - Color flash Pattern 1
  • Press Key "2" - Color flash Pattern 2
  • Press Key "z" - Angular variation Form
  • Press Key "X" - Spread Form (Mouse position - assemble & disperse)
  • Press Key "C" - Helix Form (Mouse position - assemble & disperse)
  • Press Key "S" - Rectangle Form (Mouse position - strokeweight)
  • Press Key "SPACE" - Floating Form
Try different combinations of Mode & Pattern & Form!
